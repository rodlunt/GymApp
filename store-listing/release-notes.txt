Initial release of Clean Reps - a simple workout tracker with no subscriptions, no ads, and no account required.

Features:
• Log sets, reps, and weights for any exercise
• Create custom routines that match your training style
• Set up multiple gym profiles with different equipment
• Automatic personal record tracking
• Estimated 1RM charts to track strength progress
• Weekly volume stats and 4-week trends
• Smart scheduling - the app knows which workout day is next
• Works completely offline
• Dark mode support
• Choose between kg and lbs

All data stays on your device. No servers, no tracking, no analytics.
