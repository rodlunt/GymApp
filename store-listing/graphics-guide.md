# Play Store Graphics Requirements

## 1. App Icon (Required)
**Size:** 512 x 512 px PNG (32-bit, with alpha)

Your current logo works well. You need to:
- Export `assets/logo-light.svg` at 512x512px
- Save as PNG with transparent or white background

**Tool:** Use Figma, Canva, or Inkscape to resize the SVG.


## 2. Feature Graphic (Required)
**Size:** 1024 x 500 px JPG or PNG

This is the banner shown at the top of your store listing.

**Design suggestion:**
- Teal background (#14B8A6 - your brand color)
- Your logo on the left or center
- Text: "Clean Reps" and tagline "Track. Build. Progress."
- Optional: Phone mockup showing the app

**Free tools:**
- Canva (canva.com) - has Play Store templates
- Figma (figma.com) - free for personal use


## 3. Screenshots (Required - minimum 2)
**Size:** Min 320px, Max 3840px (any side)
**Recommended:** 1080 x 1920 px (phone portrait)

You need 2-8 screenshots. Recommended screens to capture:
1. Home/Workout screen - "Start your workout in seconds"
2. Active workout with exercises - "Log sets as you go"
3. Routines list - "Build custom routines"
4. Progress/Stats screen - "See your gains over time"

**How to take good screenshots:**
1. Run app on emulator or device
2. Fill with realistic sample data (not "Test" or "asdf")
3. Take screenshot
4. Add phone frame and captions using Canva or screenshots.pro

**Free mockup tools:**
- screenshots.pro (free tier)
- mockuphone.com
- Canva templates


## 4. Optional Assets

### Tablet Screenshots
**Size:** 1200 x 1920 px (7" tablet) or 1600 x 2560 px (10" tablet)
Not required but looks professional.

### Promo Video
**Specs:** YouTube URL, 30 sec - 2 min
Not required for initial launch.
